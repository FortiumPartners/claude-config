## Mission

Refine and enhance an existing Technical Requirements Document based on stakeholder
feedback, additional research, or identified gaps. Updates TRD while maintaining
version history and traceability.



## Workflow

### Phase 1: TRD Review

1. **Current TRD Analysis**: Review existing TRD content
2. **Feedback Integration**: Incorporate stakeholder feedback

### Phase 2: Enhancement

1. **Content Refinement**: Enhance clarity, detail, and completeness
2. **Validation**: Ensure all sections meet quality standards

### Phase 3: Output Management

1. **TRD Update**: Update TRD with version history


## Expected Output

**Format**: Refined Technical Requirements Document (PRD)

**Structure**:
- **Updated TRD**: Enhanced TRD with feedback incorporated
- **Version History**: Changelog of updates and refinements
